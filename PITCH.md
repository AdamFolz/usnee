## 1. Problem

### Key Points
- **Core pain point:** People who use injectable drugs have zero safe, stigma-free tools to track consumption, identify dangerous patterns, and access emergency help — existing apps are either judgmental, data-invasive, or don't exist.
- **Current state:** Users rely on memory, paper notes, or judgmental forums. Healthcare providers get no objective data. Harm reduction organizations lack digital reach.
- **Pain point scale:** ~16 million injection drug users in Europe & CIS alone (UNODC 2024). Overdose deaths exceed 100,000/year globally (WHO). Zero dedicated digital harm-reduction tools exist in the Telegram/Mini App ecosystem.

## 2. Solution

### Key Points
- **Product positioning:** USNEE is a privacy-first Telegram Mini App that lets users track injections, analyze patterns, earn safety-positive achievements, and access emergency resources — all without judgment or data exposure.
- **Core value propositions:**
  1. **Stigma-free tracking:** Log method, site, volume, trigger, and notes with zero moral framing. Gamified harm reduction (achievements for safe intervals, rotation, filter use).
  2. **Emergency-ready:** One-tap access to hotlines, recovery position guide, naloxone info — integrated into a persistent SOS button.
  3. **Data ownership:** Encrypted local storage + optional export. No accounts, no tracking, no ads. Works entirely within Telegram's privacy envelope.
- **Differentiation:**
  - Unlike rehab apps (moralizing, abstinence-only), USNEE meets users where they are.
  - Unlike medical apps (bureaucratic, slow), USNEE is instant, mobile-native, Mini App-native.
  - Unlike note apps (passive), USNEE actively analyzes patterns and warns about dangerous intervals.

## 4. Business Model

### Key Points
- **Revenue model:**
  - **B2C Freemium (70%):** Free core tracking. Premium: advanced analytics ($4.99/mo), data export, custom reminders, cloud backup.
  - **B2B SaaS (25%):** Anonymized aggregated data licenses to NGOs, research institutions, public health departments. API access for harm-reduction organizations.
  - **Institutional (5%):** White-label deployments for government health programs, rehab center companion apps.
- **Key metrics:**
  - **Current (MVP):** 0 paying users. 2 beta testers. 12 screens built. 0 errors in build.
  - **Target Pre-A (12mo):** 50K MAU, 3% paying ($1.5K MRR), 5 B2B pilot contracts.
  - **Target Series A (24mo):** 500K MAU, 5% paying ($75K MRR), $1.2M ARR, break-even.
- **Growth flywheel:**
  - Product-led: Viral loop via Telegram sharing + achievement bragging (anonymized).
  - Community-led: Partnerships with harm-reduction NGOs for distribution.
  - Data-led: B2B revenue from aggregate insights funds free tier growth.
- **Unit economics:**
  - CAC: ~$0.15 (organic Telegram growth + NGO partnerships).
  - LTV: $45 (avg 9-month premium subscription).
  - LTV/CAC = 300:1 (exceptional for social impact apps).
